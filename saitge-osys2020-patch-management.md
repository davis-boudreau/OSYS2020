### **Instructions for Using the Worksheet**

1.  **Complete System Information**  
    Record details of the machine or environment where the patch will be applied.

2.  **Document Patch Details**  
    Include the patch identifier (KB number), type, and source to ensure authenticity.

3.  **Assess Risk**  
    Note the vulnerability addressed and its severity. This helps prioritize critical patches.

4.  **Plan Deployment**  
    Schedule the patching process, confirm testing in a safe environment, and outline rollback steps in case of failure.

5.  **Verify Installation**  
    After applying the patch, confirm successful installation and check system stability. Document any issues.

6.  **Ensure Compliance**  
    Record audit confirmation and assign responsibility for tracking future updates.
